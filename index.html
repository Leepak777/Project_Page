Watch FPS Project Demo Video FPSProject

A first-person shooter game developed using Unreal Engine 5, combining C++ and Blueprints.

Currently features a variety of weapon types including rifles, shotguns, pistols, greatswords, katanas, charge guns, and laser beams.

Multiple characters are available, with imported 3D meshes rigged via Blender for animation compatibility.

Players earn points by defeating enemies; enemies respawn once cleared to maintain gameplay flow.

This project challenged me to transition from 2D to 3D game development and helped me learn the fundamentals of Blender and Unreal Engine.

I particularly enjoyed integrating custom characters and designing immersive combat mechanics.

Future plans include:

  • Implementing combat effects like bleeding, poison, and knockback
  • Adding destructible objects
  • Improving animation systems and rigging
  • Optimizing for higher enemy counts
  • Enhancing the UI/UX
ERP
